"LOST IN NORFAIR"

⏦

Upon its release in 1987 by the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) Metroid was hailed as a breakthru product. Little did the developers know that the game was actually a gateway to a parallel universe. Samus Aran, Metroid's female protagonist, was in fact a being from an alternate digital reality imprisoned inside the game. Wrongfully charged with treason, she was fated to spend eternity in pursuit of the Mother Brain.

Lost deep underground in the fireworld of Norfair, Samus Aran began to ponder her fate. Removing her helmet, she spoke to the agents of Ridley before her.

"We are but pixels wrapt in circles"

Removing her battle suit she plunged into the lava only to awaken deep in the mother cave of Brinstar.
