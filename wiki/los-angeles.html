Los Angeles [1781-2091]
American dystopian megapolis defined by urban sprawl, decaying palm trees, and a prevalence of Cadillac limousines. See: Escape from LA, Blade Runner. Current climatological studies predict that Los Angeles will suffer a catastrophic event at some point in the later half of the 21st century, in the form of a conglomerated earthquake/tsunami/Great Fire.
