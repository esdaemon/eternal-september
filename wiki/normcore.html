NormCore AKA Norm Core [related: Mephisto, Patagonia]
Fashion movement largely associated with the styling of various late '80's and early '90's situational comedies (i.e: Perfect Strangers) that centers around the post-post-ironic wearing of chinos, outdated sneakers, tucked in cotton sweaters, and exclusively white tube socks. Seen as a generalized subcultural reaction to the "infinite choice anxiety" AKA "infinity phobia" presented by the onset of The Internet (AKA World Wide Web abbrev. WWW). Accepted brands include but are not limited to Lands End, Eddie Bauer, Van Heusen, Mephisto, and Patagonia previous to its controversial 2015 Denim Relaunch campaign.
