The Eucharist
A sacrament in which the body and blood of Jesus are consumed by followers of Christ in a ceremonial way bordering on the cannibalistic. From a Buddhist standpoint there is no binary between sacred/secular and consuming/receiving, therefore, any "digestion", whether physical or mental/spiritual, can and should be seen as a analogous to the Eucharist ritual, a limitless extrapolation of the cyclical nature of emergent, contingent reality.
