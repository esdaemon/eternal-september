Phage [related: Silurian Theory]
Robotic looking self-replicating virus composed of protein encapsulated DNA. Phage have been used experimentally by Soviet scientists to preserve food and virophage. There are thought to be around 1031 phages on earth - more than every other lifeform combined. It is estimated that over 75% of all marine bacteria is infected by phages.
