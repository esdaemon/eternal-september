Phantom City [disambiguation: Phantom Planet]
A Buddhist parable concerning the impermanence of being, as differentiated from the Californian "slacker rock" band Phantom Planet, known best for guest starring as themselves in Season 3, Ep. 8 of Sabrina the Teenage Witch. Entitled "And the Sabrina Goes to... ", the episode centers the titular character's misguided and unquenchable desire for constant recognition and reassurance resulting in her entrapment in an endless time loop at a personal awards show of her own magickal making.
