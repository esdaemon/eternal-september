Top Gun [Konami, 1987]
NES title only vaguely affiliated with the eponymous film. Commonly associated with the E.H.S.P.F., primarily due to its final scene inexplicably based in space. Notable for its brute almost E.T.-on-Atari-level abstraction, with control-based directives increasingly divested from any corresponding action as the game progresses through its four discrete levels, as exemplified by the aircraft carrier landing sequence at end Stage/Mission 2.
