Venusian Enigmata
An area of Occult1 scholarship centering the many mysterious and extraordinary facets of Venus, ex. Black Drop and Ashen Light. Many Occultists extend the concept of Venusian Enigmata to objects or areas of related name and function, ex. The Venetian2. The underlying principles of Venusian Enigmata have been extended to other planets by various practitioners, ex. Saturn's Hexagon, thus positioning it as adjacent to the Illuminati-based Silurian Hypothesis.

1Biblical scholars and meta-physicists are also active in the field of Venusian Enigmata, ex. Immanuel Velikovksy.

2The oft-flooded archipelagic city of Venice, Italy was reportedly named in honor of Venus - both rose to existence out of the sea and are commonly associated with romance, wealth, and prostitution.
