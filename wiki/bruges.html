Bruges [related: Belfry of Bruges]
Largest city of the Flemish region of Belgium. Spared from destruction in both World War I and World War II. As a result, has one of the highest densities of historically significant architecture in the world, perhaps most notably the Belfry of Bruges which houses a municipal carillon of 47 bells on an upper octagonal stage, employing a full-time carillonneur.
