Alien [related: Aliens]
Sci-fi horror film from 1979 directed by Ridley Scott prior to his work on Blade Runner. Alien centers on an acid-blooded face-fucking double-mouthed extraterrestrial species designed by the spooky Swiss recluse H.R. Giger1. The film's critically-lauded 1986 sequel - simply, Aliens - was directed by the auteur and adventurous James Cameron2.

1The second instance of Cameron taking the directorial helm of an established franchise following 1982's Piranha 2: The Spawning.

2Originally discovered and delivered from obscurity by the Chilean director and spiritual guru Alejandro Jodorowsky.
