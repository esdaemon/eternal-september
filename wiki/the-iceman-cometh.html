The Iceman Cometh [related: Jeff Bridges]
American play by [REDACTED] premiered in [REDACTED] focusing on role of self-deception AKA pipe dreams in surviving " the simulation". Featuring an ensemble cast of downtrodden men, a center-heavy venn diagram can be formed between actors featured in prominent stagings of the play and lead villians in seminal '80's films, including but not limited to Brian Deneghy, James Cromwell, Jeff Bridges, Sorrell Booke (Boss Hog from The Dukes of Hazzard), and the guy from Total Recall and Robocop. Ironically, a fixed-media version of The Iceman Cometh has never been successfully executed1.

1With the notable exception of Cardboard Computer's launching of a short-form 8-bit video game platform entitled The Entertainment as part of its Kentucky Route Series that heavily references numerous aspects of the play.
