Imbruglia AKA Natalie Jane Imbruglia [Feb. 4, 1975]
The creator of "Torn", a 1997 song outlining the dissolution of early romantic ideation into systematized apathy resulting in aesthetic-based hopelessness and depression paralleling the development and subsequent unraveling of mid/late '90's neo-liberal tech-oriented utopianism:

"I can see the perfect sky is torn".
