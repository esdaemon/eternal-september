Elysium AKA Elysian Fields
An expansive and exceedingly pleasant section of the Greek underworld reserved for the righteous and heroic. Separated from the den of Hades by the stream of Okeanos, the shady parks of the Elysian Fields provide an ideal setting for its chosen residents to engage in various leisure pursuits including music performance, poetry recitation, recreational athletics, and consensual orgies. Comparable to Valhalla in Norse mythology and the idyllic plains in the neighborhood of Memphis containing the capital tombs of Egypt.
