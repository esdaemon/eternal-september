Bland Axiom

Cincinnati you are an endless white space
a non-chain roadside motel version of a mindfuck
a personal/mythical end of times

[ANESTHETIZED CLASSROOM/EARLY AFTERNOON1]

The undergraduates love to be scolded:

“Your music sounds like microchip vomit.
Reminds me of time spent living inside
my own mind

of topaz Pontiacs with burgundy
interiors parked at empty
strip malls in rain.”

[blushes]

“First, you must rid yourself of Mephisto.”

[looks sheepishly downward]

“Your work is secondary to some hidden
primal aim.”


Cincinnati, you are no place for marijiuana.
An entire city bathed in gymnasium light.


There is no future only ocean waves
I take my palms and press them at angles

(I’ve learned to do this quietly and with
soft purpose. Learned to think of them2
as animals.)

“Remember the words of Stockhausen when
composing for strings and helicopter.”
