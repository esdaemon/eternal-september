Silver Walls

It seems unlikely
that so many inanimate objects
could be out to fuck with me.

When it rains
the streetlights
they serve as portals

The corners of my mindscape
move edgewise in a circular
motion. Sudden tectonic visions
strange like the very end of Contra1,
saying:

    do things
like they do them
     in Jazz2

I try but cannot locate center.

I am this close to moving to Buffalo.


It’s Saturday night
I color it beige
there’s strange music
behind every door.

I see the river and
imagine it sclasseways
doubled up like two
silver walls.


      Why bother with verisimilitude?


When the end comes
I’ll be in Bruges.
