Phantom City

There is a man in the restaurant
who talks like drunk Jeff Bridges
saying "mister, this is not a tennis
match" and "go fish" and we sit
quietly in the space between
what he sees and what he
comments on.


And I don't know what he means
when he says we are: "afflicted"

And I don't know what he means when he says
he is: "allergic to the saxophone"

The walk to the car is angular and vague.
Containing within it a multitude of
probable outcomes.

I am trying to get a bit more scientific about it:

"We are lines, on a grid

making

patterns
rose

patterns"

Tell me how the streetlights feel
when pressed into your eye parts.


(A softness like after bathing)

Concussed but still driving
I circle each cul-de-sac
carefully as if with child
